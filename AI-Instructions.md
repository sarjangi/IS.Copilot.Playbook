# AI Instructions: A Simple Guide

## 1. What an AI Instruction Is

An instruction is simply a task description written in natural language that tells the AI what result you want.

**Example:**

Translate this sentence into Spanish: "How are you?"

**Structure of a basic instruction:**

| Part | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Task | What the AI should do |
| Input | The information to work with |
| Output | What format the result should be |

**Example:**

- **Task:** Summarize
- **Input:** Article text
- **Output:** 3 bullet points

---

## 2. Why Instructions Are Important

AI models do not "think" like humans. They predict text based on patterns learned during training.

**Good instructions:**
- reduce confusion
- guide the model
- improve accuracy

**Bad instructions cause:**
- vague answers
- wrong format
- hallucinations

---

## 3. The Basic Components of Good Instructions

### 1?? Clear task

Tell the AI exactly what to do.

**Bad:**

Tell me about space.

**Better:**

Explain black holes in simple terms for a 10-year-old.

### 2?? Context

Provide background information.

**Example:**

You are a biology teacher. Explain photosynthesis to a high school student.

This technique is widely used in prompt engineering.

### 3?? Output format

Tell the AI how to structure the result.

**Example:**

Summarize this article in:
- 3 bullet points
- each under 20 words

---

## 4. Common Instruction Styles

- **Direct instruction**
  - List 5 benefits of exercise.
- **Role-based instruction**
  - You are a professional nutritionist. Suggest a healthy breakfast.
- **Step-by-step instruction**
  - Solve the math problem step by step.

---

## 5. Instruction Patterns Often Used

**Pattern 1 Ś Task + Input**

Translate to French:

Hello, how are you?

**Pattern 2 Ś Role + Task**

You are a historian. Explain the causes of World War I.

Related to the study of World War I.

**Pattern 3 Ś Task + Constraints**

Write a summary in exactly 50 words.

---

## 6. Instruction Hierarchy (Used in AI Systems)

Many AI systems follow layers of instructions:

- System instructions ¢ rules set by developers
- User instructions ¢ prompts from users
- Context instructions ¢ information from documents or conversation

The AI prioritizes them in that order.

---

## 7. Simple Example of a Well-Structured Instruction

**Role:** You are a travel guide.

**Task:** Recommend a 3-day itinerary.

**Location:** Tokyo

**Output:**
- Day-by-day schedule
- 5 places per day

---

## 8. Where Instructions Are Used

Instructions are used in many AI tasks:

- chatbots
- coding assistants
- document summarization
- translation
- research tools
- automation

These systems rely heavily on techniques from machine learning.
